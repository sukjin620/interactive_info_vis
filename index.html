ECG Digital Clock

This sketch visualises time by creating an interactive ECG monitor that pulses with each passing second and a clickable heart that beats every second if triggered.

  • Context: This is designed for hospital wards. It serves both as a functional clock and a calming visual element that aligns with the medical environment, helping patients and visitors track time while providing a soothing, rhythmic animation.
  • Design decisions:
    • Three-tier vertical layout: Time display at top, scrolling ECG in the middle, and pulsing heart icon at bottom creates clear visual hierarchy that's readable from a distance, important for waiting rooms where viewers may be seated far from displays.
    • Soft medical color palette: Light blue-gray background (#F5FAFC) with gentle red accents (#DC3234) evokes medical equipment while remaining calming and non-alarming—crucial for reducing anxiety in healthcare settings.
    • Amplified minute pulses: Making the pulse 1.8x larger at minute intervals provides clear time markers for patients tracking appointment times or medication schedules without adding visual clutter or numbers.
  • Future work: We could maybe add hourly chimes or gentle notification sounds for medication reminders that can be customized per room.

Hospital Clock

This is a classic square clock but desigend with some hospital themed animation.

  • Context: This clock is designed for hospital environments like nurse stations, or patient rooms. It functions as both a timekeeping device and a subtle visual display that reflects the rhythm of hospital shifts and patient monitoring systems.
  • Design decisions:
    • Ambulance center icon:The traditional center dot is replaced with a minimalist ambulance icon, symbolizing emergency response and hospital readiness—serving as both a branding and contextual cue.
    • Shift-based color transitions: The clock color changes its tint to reflect shift times (day, evening, night), to help hospital staff subconsciously track time cycles within long shifts.
    • Red cross background: The medical symbolic background gives it the feel of a Red Cross hospital wall clock.
  • Future work: If there're more time, we can try making the ambulance moves following the pointer to make it more interesting.

Hospital Clock

This is a heart-shaped digital clock that beats in rhythm with each passing second.

  • Context: This clock combines timekeeping with a visual heartbeat metaphor, creating a connection between the passage of time and the rhythm of life. It could serve in wellness spaces, healthcare settings, or personal devices where emotional connection to time matters..
  • Design decisions:
    • Heart shaped:This is to create a smooth, symmetrical curves that give it an authentic appearance.
    • Heartbeat animation: This shows a smooth heartbeat that synchronizes with each second.
    • 12-hour format: Using am/pm is more intuitive and familiar to most users.
  • Future work: We can add heartbeat sound effects to enhance the heartbeat experience.
